About Sarah

The beginning of my professional career was spent learning an array of skills from some of the top stylists and artists in New York City. Although the world may have perceived me as a "barber" I considered myself a 3-D designer of sorts. Thoughout my studies, the way our bodies intereact with our environment, (specifically our hair) intrigued me. And like the Grade A millenial that I am, it led me to investigate the way we interact with our bodies on social media and the internet.

With a new found passion for the way we interact with the World Wide Web and the way that media is consumed I hope to successfully make the move from fashion to media.

Focused in the development of IOS and Web Applications
